Rent and Loan Outdoor Gear

with the Outdoor Equipment Exchange mobile app

Outdoor Equipment Exchange is a mobile app that lets you rent and loan used outdoor gear from other users. Find and share a variety of items and connect with gear owners/renters before meeting up to exchange items.

You can also send and receive payments, keep track of return dates, request renewals, and message other users—all in one centralized platform.

Rent gear on a budget

Borrowing gear is a great alternative if you are trying a new activity or looking for a budget-friendly option.

Loan your gear for others to rent

Do you have rarely used equipment? In a few taps, you can post it for rent for others to enjoy.

View renter and owner reviews

Ratings and reviews (for both renters and owners) can help you find people you can trust.

Features

Sign up and choose your favourite activities

Creating an account only takes a few easy steps. After signing up, you can select your favourite outdoor activities to make it easier to discover items that are relevant to you. You can edit your preferences later if you pick up a new hobby in the future.

Browse and search gear

Your home feed is tailored to your favourite activities. Sort and filter listings to match your ideal price range, travel distance, or activity.

Need something specific? Search for a specific activity or type of equipment. You can also view your recent searches, save search terms, and allow notifications for new posts that fit your search category.

Rent an item

Found an item you like? Choose your preferred pick up and return dates and add the item to your cart.

Paying through the app helps ensure that you are getting consistent prices with no surprise charges. Your billing information will be kept secure.

Post an item for loan

Own equipment you rarely use? Post it to Outdoor Equipment Exchange for others to rent.

Add an image, a name, a short description, and the item’s condition. Describing your item can encourage potential renters who are curious about your gear.

You also have the option to charge late fees to help ensure that you receive your items on time.

Manage your posts and rentals in one place

Keep track of your items in one place whether you are a renter, a gear owner, or both.

Gear owners can view and edit their posts and see which items are currently on loan or available. Renters can find their current rentals, view upcoming return dates, and request renewals.

You can also find your Favourite items that you’ve saved for later.

Ratings and reviews

User profiles let you see others’ ratings and reviews from renters and gear owners they’ve exchanged items with. This can help you find people whom you feel like you can trust.

After you rent or loan an item to someone, you can leave a review to tell others about your experience.

In-app messaging

Contact an owner or renter to learn more about an item, discuss rental dates, or arrange a meeting time.

Messaging through the app means you don’t need to share your phone number or other personal contact information.
